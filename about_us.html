Daddy B & Me is a collaboration of Lauren Bauerlein and her father, Matt, who create rustic designs that are a perfect addition to any home. Matt, a technology and shop teacher from Victor, NY, has been woodworking for a number of years. With the addition of Lauren's knack for design from a young age, the combination is nothing short of wonderful.

"A father-daughter crafting duo"

With a rustic themed mindset, many items Daddy B & Me create are constructed from used pallets and barn wood. Using these items allow for a truly unique expercience from once piece to the next. Not only will our customers to own a one of a kind item, but also provides true artistic creativity with each project.
